Lockd

A browser extension that locks you in. Surgical focus blocking + Pomodoro sessions you can't casually quit.

Chromium + Firefox · Manifest V3 · No accounts · No tracking

01 · THE CORE IDEA

Surgical blocking

Most blockers are blunt — block youtube.com and you lose the tutorial you actually needed. Lockd removes the rabbit holes while keeping the site usable.

🎯

Element-level presets

Curated, per-site CSS that hides distraction elements during focus — each one a toggle. Search and the thing you came for stay usable; on breaks everything reappears.

SiteWhat it trims
YouTubeHome feed · Recommendations sidebar · Shorts shelves & nav · End-screen suggestions · Comments optional
InstagramReels tab · Explore tab · Suggested posts optional
X / Twitter"For You" tab · Trending & who-to-follow · Explore nav
RedditHome / popular feed · Popular & All nav
🚫

Whole-site blocking

Blocklist mode blocks a set of domains (ships with TikTok & Facebook). Allowlist mode blocks everything except what you allow. Blocked navigations hit an in-extension block page that auto-steps aside when your break starts.

📺

YouTube channel lock

Allow one specific channel — a lofi stream, a course — and block every other channel and video during focus. Accepts @handles, /channel/UC… IDs, or URLs.

02 · FOCUS

Sessions & always-on

⏱️

Pomodoro loop

Pick a duration (1 / 2 / 4 / 6h) and rhythm (25/5, 50/10, 90/15). Work blocks lock; breaks unlock — so rest feels earned.

♾️

Always-on focus

A single toggle that keeps blocking active without a timed session.

🛟

Resilient by design

Sessions use absolute timestamps + alarms, so they survive the browser putting the extension to sleep or a mid-session reload. Reopen and the timer is exactly where it should be.

03 · WORK SURFACES

Calm & Command

🌙

Calm

One giant timer, nothing else. The zen-study surface — just the work and the time.

🛰️

Command

Mission control: the timer flanked by live stat tiles and a 28-day focus wall. Momentum made tangible.

Plus a count toggle — show time left (countdown) or time focused (count-up).

05 · THE LOCK

Friction, honesty & privacy

🔒

Deliberate exit friction

Ending early is intentionally hard — the give-up path needs both an 8-second countdown and a typed phrase. Pause stays soft; quitting costs something.

🛡️

Private by default

No accounts, no tracking, no network calls of its own. Sessions, settings, and stats live in your browser's local storage.

!

Honest limit: Lockd works in your browser. It can't lock native apps or your phone — that's an extension's boundary, and the doom-scrolling mostly happens in the browser anyway.

06 · POLISH & REACH

Design & browser support

🎨

Two themes

Dark (CyberX) and Light (Noteflow), switchable and persisted. A serious tool that happens to be beautiful.

🌐

Cross-browser

One codebase, two builds — Chromium (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc) and Firefox — on Manifest V3.
